About Gehad

Guangzhou Gehad Trading Co., Ltd is a company specializing in the design and sales of various labels and functional materials. With professional expertise and global operational sales capabilities, we provide high-quality, sustainable sales channel solutions to customers worldwide.

As the exclusive distributor of RotoMetrics products in China, we are committed to bringing world-class precision die-cutting solutions to the Chinese market.

Our Strengths

Why Choose Us

60 Years of Industry Leadership

RotoMetrics is an internationally renowned company with decades of experience in manufacturing die-cutting tools. For 60 years, it has maintained its leadership position as a pioneer in rotary converting and a global partner in the processing industry.

Its magnetic die blades and magnetic cylinders offer higher precision (micron-level), longer service life, and more advanced manufacturing processes compared to common market alternatives. The tool surface can achieve a 73-75 hardness coating.

RotoMetrics is a supplier and partner to several of the world's top printing equipment companies, earning customer trust through strong local manufacturing capabilities, extensive product development experience, and exceptional customer service.
