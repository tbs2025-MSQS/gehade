Dead-Shaft Idler Roll Mounts

Three universal mounting designs: quick-type, split-flange type, or rigid mount type. Webex provides three simple solutions for quick and easy installation of Webex idler rolls. Easily mounted to the machine frame side panel or other machine framework. The mount design holds and secures all standard dead-shaft idler rolls. Quick-type mount — a simple idler shaft mount with sufficient flexibility for positioning, leveling, and proper alignment.

Webex Thermal Transfer Rolls (Cooling Rolls)

Cost-effective, industry-leading solution ensuring strict temperature control. Utilizing a unique gain-variable pitch channel design to control temperature variation across the entire product and overall heat transfer performance. Proprietary computational analysis ensures mechanical and thermal performance. ASME certification options available.

For over 40 years, with our precision thermal and cooling rolls, Webex has maintained industry-leading levels of continuous, economical, and efficient temperature control. Our engineers, drawing on extensive industry experience and proprietary analysis processes, can precisely simulate thermal roll performance prior to manufacturing.
